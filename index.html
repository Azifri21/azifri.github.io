Series

Terra Prima

A researcher, a collector of emotions, and a madman, are dragged into conspiracies and legends that span across the multiverse.

Genre(s): science-fiction, drama, action/adventure, space opera.

Themes: free will vs. determinism, individuality vs. collectivism, personal identity, ethics, history and legacy, human emotion.

Format: Comic

Publication status: Ongoing

Children of the Meteorite

A crystal hunter and her students race to save a town from a swarm of monsters while uncovering the truth behind the world's collapse.

Genre(s): science-fiction, adventure, post-apocalyptic, speculative, weird fiction.

Themes: survival, found family, cosmic horror, human interdependence.

Format: Novel

Publication status: Ongoing

Short stories

Grandma's Hometown

Carmen and Rika explore an abandoned extraterrestrial colony, which may have changed quite a bit since people last lived there.

Genre(s): science-fiction, comedy.

Themes: exploration, lost technology, family history, Halloween

Format: Comic

Publication status: Completed

Stop on Request

A hiker goes for a walk and ends up running.

Genre(s): comedy.

Themes: nature, exploration, local history.

Format: Comic

Publication status: Completed

Adventure in New World

Jack and Mike are childhood friends whose bond is put to the test when Mike and his family have to move far away. Through video games and their imaginations, they're determined to prove that some friendships are too strong to disconnect.

Story by David L. and comic adaptation by me.

Genre(s): gaming, isekai.

Themes: friendship, imagination, adventure.

Format: Comic

Publication status: Completed
