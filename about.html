About me

I'm Azifri, or Cristina Villafranca, an author and biologist. I particularly enjoy science fiction, adventure, and magic realism. My aim is to create stories that are fun to read while also exploring topics that I consider interesting or relevant.


My stories are developed either as comics or in prose, depending on which format I consider best fits the idea. While most can be read for free, they are also available for purchase as physical books or in digital format (PDF and EPUB). Life is hard enough, so if my stories can bring a little joy into someone's life, then the goal is achieved.
